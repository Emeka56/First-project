Learn how to use step by step

Learn how to create,store and share files on decentralized storage technologies
with the frictionless exrperience you expect in a morden work flow.


1. Create an account

when you create a DeuploadAccount we ask for some personal infor.
By profiding accurate infor, you can help keep your account secure and make our services more useful.


  1. Click Create Account button on header.
  2. Enter your email Address
  3. In the 'Full name' feild, enter your name.
  4. Enter and confirm your password.
  5. Click and create your Account.

2. Upload files from desktop

you can upload, view, share and edit files with Deupload.
when you upload a fike to Deupload, it will take up space in your disk,
even if your upload to a folder owned by someone else.


  1. on your computer, go to drive.google.com
  2. At the top-left, click New and then upload files or upload folder.
  3. Choose the file or folder you want to upload.

3. Retrieve data Uploaded

All data stored using Deupload is made available for retrieval via IPFS, the interPlanetory File System.
IPFS is a distributted, peer to peer network for sharing content-addressed data.


  1. Click on any files and folder
  2. click on infor icon at top-left to see details.
  3. copy CID or IPFS link to use on your NFT.

4. Share files and Folders

You can control whether people can edit or can only view your files and folders
when you share them and even after you share them.
Edits made to a file shared or folder are updates live for everyone there've been shared with.


  1. Hover over the file or folder you'd like to share.
  2. click on the share icon at menu.
  3. type in the email ou'd like to share with.
  4. select can edit or can view.
  5. click generate link for your folder file.

5. Convert to team folder

Team folders help admins manage team files and folders.
You can share teaam folders with specifc group.
You can convert any folder to to a team folder and the folder willbe shared with the folder members only.


  1. Hover over the file or folder you'd like to share.
  2. click on the share icon at menu.
  3. type in the email ou'd like to share with.
  4. select can edit or can view.
  5. click generate link for your folder file.
